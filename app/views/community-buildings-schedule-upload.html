Attach community buildings spreadsheet

You have selected to claim a repayment of tax on Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme (GASDS) donations collected in your community building(s). If you do not want to make this type of claim, you can remove it by answering ‘No’ to the question 'Are you claiming for donations collected in community buildings?’ in the Claim details page.

The community buildings schedule is for small donations of £30 or less that were collected by a charity in a community building. CASCs cannot claim under the community buildings rules.

To make a claim, you must fill out a GASDS community buildings spreadsheet. Your claim spreadsheet must include details of the community building(s) in which the donations were collected and the amounts collected.

We recommend you read the commnuity buildings spreadsheet guidance before you complete and save your spreadsheet.

Complete and attach a spreadsheet

  1. Download a community buildings spreadsheet.
  2. Complete the spreadsheet and save it as an Open Document Spreadsheet (.ods) file to your computer.
  3. Select ‘Choose file’ and select the saved spreadsheet file from your computer to attach it to this claim.
  4. Select ‘Next’.
