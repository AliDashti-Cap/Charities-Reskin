Help about: Charity regulator

Help about: Charity registration number

Where you have selected a regulator from the drop-down list you must enter the registration number the regulator issued to you (this does not apply where you have selected "none" or "other").

The registration number from the regulator will be on any paperwork received from that regulator.

If the charity is dual registered, please include a registration number you have used in previous correspondence with HMRC or complete and submit a change of details form (ChV1) with this information before making your claim.
