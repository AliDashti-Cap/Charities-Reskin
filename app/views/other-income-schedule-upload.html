CHARITY TC088
HMRC charities reference: XR4010

Attach other income spreadsheet

You have selected to claim a repayment of tax on other income donations. If you do not want to make this type of claim, you can remove it by answering ‘No’ to the question ‘Are you claiming UK tax deducted from other income?’ in the Claim details page.

Other income is a repayment of tax from:

  • bank and building society interest
  • interest from government stocks
  • income from wayleaves, for example, sums paid by a utility company for the right to put cables across a piece of land
  • royalties
  • other types of annual payment
  • estate income
  • discretionary trust income

To make a claim, you must fill out a other income spreadsheet. Your claim must be supported by details of each payer, the gross value of the payment and the tax deducted from each payment.

We recommend you read the other income spreadsheet guidance before you complete and save your spreadsheet.

Complete and attach a spreadsheet

  1. Download a other income spreadsheet.
  2. Complete the spreadsheet and save it as an Open Document Spreadsheet (.ods) file to your computer.
  3. Select ‘Choose file’ and select the saved spreadsheet file from your computer to attach it to this claim.
  4. Select ‘Next’.
