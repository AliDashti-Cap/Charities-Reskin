Make a charity repayment claim

You can also make a claim using commercial software.

Before you start a claim

You need to complete a spreadsheet to make a claim on:

  • Gift Aid donations
  • tax relief you are claiming back on other income
  • Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme (GASDS) connected charities donations
  • GASDS community buildings donations

You do not need to submit a spreadsheet to claim GASDS donations that were not collected in a community building.

Before you start your claim, we reccomend that you download and complete the appropriate spreadsheet, based on what you want to claim for.

There is guidance available on claiming Gift Aid as a charity or community amateur sports club (CASC). For further guidance, contact Charities helpline on telephone: 0300 123 1073.
