You can also make a charity repayment claim using commercial software.

Before you start a claim

You need to complete a spreadsheet to make a claim on:

  • Gift Aid donations
  • tax relief you are claiming back on ‘other income’
  • connected charities donations
  • community buildings donations

Before you start your claim, download and complete the appropriate spreadsheet, based on what you want to claim for.

Each spreadsheet contains instructions to help you fill it in correctly.

You can include a maximum of 1,000 donations in each spreadsheet.

There is guidance available on claiming Gift Aid as a charity or community amateur sports club (CASC).
