Help about: Claiming - Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme

Select 'Yes' if you want to claim under the Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme (GASDS). This is separate from your Gift Aid claim.

The GASDS is for small cash donations of £20 or less that the charity or Community Amateur Sports Club has received that you do not have a Gift Aid declaration for. It only applies to donations you have already received since 6 April 2013.

You will be asked to enter the total amount of all small cash donations that you have received and are claiming for under the GASDS on this occasion.

Please note: In general, you can claim a maximum of £1,250 of top-up payments in any one tax year for donations received from 6 April 2013 onwards.

Help about: Donations – Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme (GASDS)

Only select ‘Yes’ if you are claiming under the Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme (GASDS) on donations of £20 or less that were not collected in a community building since 6 April 2014.

If you are claiming under the community buildings rule only, you must answer ‘No’ to this question.

Gift Aid Small Donations (GASDS) received in the 2013/14 tax year are now ineligible, as they should have been claimed for by 5 April 2016.

To prevent your payment being delayed - Please check below

  1. Don’t enter amounts above the maximum allowance for GASDS

    2. When completing your claim, do not enter an amount above the maximum small donation limit of £5000 (on donations collected in the 14/15 and 15/16 tax year) or £8000 (from April 2016 onwards),

    For example, if you collected £5800 in the 2015/16 tax year, you should enter £5000 as the amount collected.

  2. Enter the correct tax year

    3. You must ensure that you enter the correct tax year for the small donations you are claiming for.

    GASDS are claimed by tax year, not on your accounting periods.

    Failure to do this may lead to your claim being disallowed if the system determines you have claimed on your limit for that year already.

  3. Are you eligible for Gift Aid Small Donations?

    4. Your charity or Community Amateur Sports Club must have claimed Gift Aid:

    • in at least 2 of the last 4 tax years (but without a 2 year gap between the claims) AND
    • without getting a penalty in the last 2 tax years

    If you do not meet these requirements then unfortunately, you are not eligible to claim under Small Donations.

Help about: Donations – Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme (GASDS) - Community buildings

Only select ‘Yes’ if you are claiming under the Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme on donations of £20 or less that were collected in a community building during charitable activities from 6 April 2014.

Please note: To claim under community buildings, you need to attach your completed HM Revenue & Customs schedule of community buildings when requested during the claim process.

The spreadsheet can be downloaded from the GOV.UK website. Please ensure you complete all relevant fields on the spreadsheet.

Gift Aid Small Donations (GASDS) received in the 2013/14 tax year are now ineligible, as they should have been claimed for by 5 April 2016.

Community Amateur Sports Clubs (CASCS) cannot claim under the community buildings rules.

    3. You must ensure that you enter the correct tax year for the small donations you are claiming for.

    GASDS are claimed by tax year, not on your accounting periods.

    Failure to do this may lead to your claim being disallowed if the system determines you have claimed on your limit for that year already.

  3. Are you eligible for Gift Aid Small Donations?

    4. Your charity or Community Amateur Sports Club must have claimed Gift Aid:

    • in at least 2 of the last 4 tax years (but without a 2 year gap between the claims) AND

Help about: Connected Charities

Choose 'Yes' if the charity or Community Amateur Sports Club (CASC) is connected to another charity or CASC under the Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme rules. The connected charities and CASCs that claim a top-up payment for the year will share the maximum £1,250 payments limit for the tax year.

Please note: To submit your claim, you'll need to have your completed HM Revenue and Customs schedule of connected charities (prepared by your charity for the Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme) ready to attach when requested during the claim process.

You can download the schedule as a spreadsheet from the GOV.UK website. All connected charity details which are required entries on the spreadsheet should be completed.

Help about: Adjustment to previous GASDS claim

Select 'Yes' if an error was made in a previous claim which caused the charity or Community Amateur Sports Club to receive an over-payment of Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme to which it is not entitled. The over-claimed amount will, where possible, be deducted from this claim.

Where this is not possible, if for example the over paid amount is more than the repayment claim you are making, then you will need to make an additional payment to HM Revenue & Customs.
