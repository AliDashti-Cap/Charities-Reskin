You have indicated that you would like to claim a top-up payment under the Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme (GASDS) on small cash donations you have received and that your organisation is connected to one or more charities or Community Amateur Sports Clubs (CASCs) under the GASDS rules. Your claim must be supported by the name(s) and HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) charities reference number(s) of these charities or CASCs you are connected to.

You must have already completed and saved an HMRC connected charities schedule on your computer with details of your connected charities or CASCs on to support this claim.

Please note: If you haven't already done so follow the link below to download the HMRC connected charities schedule.

To complete the schedule now you will need to log out of this service. Any information you have already entered will be saved.

Once you have completed the schedule you will be able to log back into this service and attach it to your claim.

Further information about connected charities schedules can be found in the online guidance. HMRC recommend you read this guidance before you complete and save your schedule.
