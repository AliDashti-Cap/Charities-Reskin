CHARITY TC088
HMRC charities reference: XR4010

Attach connected charities spreadsheet

You have selected to claim a repayment of tax on connected charities donations. If you do not want to make this type of claim, you can remove it by answering ‘No’ to the question ‘Are you connected to any other charities or CASCs for the purpose of GASDS?’ in the Claim details page.

Charities and CASCs are generally connected under GASDS rules where one or more of the following applies:

  • one charity controls another
  • two or more charities are under common control
  • control links a number of charities - for example, if Charity A controls Charity B and Charity B controls Charity C, then all 3 charities are connected

If connected charities or CASCs have different purposes or activities, then they will not be considered a connected charity under GASDS rules. The connected charities and CASCs must share the maximum GASDS donation claim limit of £8,000.

To make a claim, you must fill out a connected charities spreadsheet. Your claim must include details of your connected charities or CASCs.

We recommend you read the connected charities spreadsheet guidance before you complete and save your spreadsheet.

Complete and attach a spreadsheet

  1. Download a connected charities spreadsheet.
  2. Complete the spreadsheet and save it as an Open Document Spreadsheet (.ods) file to your computer.
  3. Select ‘Choose file’ and select the saved spreadsheet file from your computer to attach it to this claim.
  4. Select ‘Next’.
