Make a Gift Aid claim

You have selected to claim a repayment of tax on Gift Aid donations. Gift Aid is a repayment of tax on donations from individuals who have made a valid Gift Aid declaration.

To make a claim, you must fill out a Gift Aid claim spreadsheet. Your claim spreadsheet must include full details of the donors and their donations.

We recommend you read the Gift Aid spreadsheet guidance before you complete and save your spreadsheet.

Upload a Gift Aid spreadsheet

To complete and upload a Gift Aid spreadsheet:

  1. Download a Gift Aid schedule.
  2. Complete the spreadsheet and save the file to your computer.
  3. Select ‘Choose file’ and select the saved spreadsheet file from your computer to attach it to this claim.
  4. Select ‘Next’.
