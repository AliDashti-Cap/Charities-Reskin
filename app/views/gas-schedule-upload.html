Attach Gift Aid spreadsheet

You have selected to claim a repayment of tax on Gift Aid donations. If you do not want to make this type of claim, you can remove it by answering ‘No’ to the question ‘Are you claiming Gift Aid?’ in the Claim details page.

Gift Aid is a repayment of tax on donations from individuals who have made a valid Gift Aid declaration.

To make a claim, you must fill out a Gift Aid claim spreadsheet. Your claim spreadsheet must include full details of the donors and their donations.

We recommend you read the Gift Aid spreadsheet guidance before you complete and save your spreadsheet.

Complete and attach a spreadsheet

  1. Download a Gift Aid spreadsheet.
  2. Complete the spreadsheet and save it as an Open Document Spreadsheet (.ods) file to your computer.
  3. Select ‘Choose file’ and select the saved spreadsheet file from your computer to attach it to this claim.
  4. Select ‘Next’.
